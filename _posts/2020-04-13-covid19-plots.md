These plots were created using Python and Plotly.  

### Context
Exploring effectiveness of mask use amongst various countries in reducing COVID-19.
- [Face Masks Against COVID-19: An Evidence Review](https://www.preprints.org/manuscript/202004.0203/v1)
- [Masks for all? The science says yes.](https://www.fast.ai/2020/04/13/masks-summary/) by Professor Trisha Greenhalgh OBE and Jeremy Howard
